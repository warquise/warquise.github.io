About Me

My Introduction

Hi 👋 My name is Marquise Simpson from Fayetteville, North Carolina and I'm a second-year honors Information Technology student at the University of North Carolina A & T State University. What drew me to the IT field was a combination of fascination with technology and a desire to be at the forefront of innovation. Ever since I was a child, I’ve been fascinated by computers. I vividly remember when, at ten years old, my parents bought me my first computer—a bulky, second-hand desktop that became my gateway to an exciting new world. One day, I accidentally downloaded a virus that caused strange pop-ups and rendered the computer almost unusable. Determined to fix it, I spent hours researching viruses and malware, learning from cybersecurity forums. Following their advice, I successfully cleaned the computer and restored its functionality, feeling a great sense of accomplishment. This experience ignited my passion for understanding and preventing cyber threats. Over the years, I delved deeper into network security, teaching myself coding, experimenting with security software, and setting up a home network for practice. Today, as I pursue my degree in Information Technology, my childhood curiosity has evolved into a clear career path. I am committed to becoming a network security professional, dedicated to safeguarding information and ensuring a safer digital world.
